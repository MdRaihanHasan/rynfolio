## Quick SEO Checklist for Each Post

- Put the main keyword in the **title, URL slug, and first paragraph**.
- Answer the searcher's problem **in the first 100 words** — don't bury it.
- Add at least one **code block, command, or screenshot** (signals first-hand experience).
- Use **H2/H3 subheadings** that match how people phrase follow-up questions.
- Link **between your own related posts** (e.g. Laravel posts link to the AWS deploy post).
- Add a short **author bio** linking to your portfolio — builds topical authority over time.
- Aim for posts where you describe a **real fix you applied** — those are hardest to copy and rank best.
